## Core Concept: The Advantage Formula

Here's the framework:


You don't build advantage from scratch. You identify what's already there and amplify it.

### Three Sources of Advantage:


These are capabilities you've developed over the past 5 years:
- Professional skills (your job expertise)
- Soft skills (communication, leadership, organization)
- Technical skills (tools, platforms, software)
- Creative skills (writing, design, strategy)



These are assets already in your possession:
- Network (contacts, relationships, communities)
- Platforms (access to tools, software, systems)
- Credentials (certifications, degrees, training)
- Experience (projects, results, case studies)
- Content (past work, writing, presentations)



These are activities you'd do even without payment:
- Hobbies you spend time on
- Topics you read about voluntarily
- Conversations you enjoy having
- Problems you love solving

## Actionable Steps: Build Your Advantage Inventory

### Step 1: Skills Inventory (20 minutes)

Set a timer. Write down every skill you've developed in the past 5 years. Don't filter. Don't judge. Just list.

- What do you do at work that comes easily to you?
- What have friends/colleagues asked you to help them with?
- What have you been paid for (or could be)?
- What tools/platforms do you use confidently?


### Step 2: Resources Inventory (15 minutes)

List everything you have access to:

- How many genuine professional contacts do you have?
- What communities are you part of?
- Who would take your call/email?

- What software do you have access to through work?
- What subscriptions do you already pay for?
- What platforms do you have accounts on?

- What certifications do you hold?
- What notable projects have you completed?
- What results can you document?


### Step 3: Interests Inventory (15 minutes)

Be honest. What do you genuinely enjoy?

- What topics do you read about voluntarily?
- What YouTube videos do you watch?
- What conversations energize you?
- What problems do you love solving?
- What would you do even if nobody paid you?


### Step 4: Advantage Ranking (20 minutes)

Now, score your top items from each list:

| Item | Type | Proficiency (1-5) | Market Demand (1-5) | Passion (1-5) | Total | Priority |
|------|------|-------------------|---------------------|---------------|-------|----------|
| Email marketing | Skill | 5 | 5 | 4 | 15 | High |
| Team training | Skill | 4 | 4 | 5 | 13 | High |

- **Proficiency:** How good are you? (1 = beginner, 5 = expert)
- **Market Demand:** Will people pay for this? (1 = niche, 5 = high demand)
- **Passion:** Do you enjoy this? (1 = drain, 5 = energizing)
